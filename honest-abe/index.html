Abraham "Honest Abe" Lincoln

President of the United States of America - 1861 to 1865

Four score and seven years ago our fathers brought forth on this continent, a new nation, conceived in Liberty, and dedicated to the proposition that all men are created equal.

Now we are engaged in a great civil war, testing whether that nation, or any nation so conceived and so dedicated, can long endure. We are met on a great battle-field of that war. We have come to dedicate a portion of that field, as a final resting place for those who here gave their lives that that nation might live. It is althogether fitting and proper that we should do this.

Accomplishments

  • 16th President
  • 1st Republican President
  • Resided over Civil War
  • Issued Emancipation Proclamation, abolishing slavery

Miscellany

  • Passed Bar Exam - 1836
  • Honoray Degrees: Princton (1864), Columbia (1861), Knox College (1860)
  • House of Representatives - 1846 to 1849
  • Patented a Device for Lifting Boats Over Shoals
